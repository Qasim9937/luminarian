The

The Luminarian Fellowship inspires and connects visionary leaders who embrace innovation and innovation management as a strategic imperative within their businesses.

Founded as an exclusive collaboration featuring Innovera, the expertise of world-class Stanford University faculty, industry pioneers, and global leaders.

The invitation-only Fellowship prepares leaders with the resources and network necessary to transform ideas into action that will shape the future and drive the right outcomes for long term growth and sustainability.

A Purpose-Driven Mission

The Luminarian Fellowship is founded upon four key tenets we believe are necessary to support the dynamic nature of innovation leadership.

Ingenuity

The quality of being resourceful lies in making tools and intelligence accessible. We believe that fostering diverse, cross-industry collaboration sparks creative solutions and new perspectives, which are imperative for driving global growth.

Intelligence

"A rising tide lifts all boats." Cultivating an esteemed, mission-driven community of global leaders, united by a shared vision for transformative impact, is crucial for uniting the best ideas, which in turn lead to impactful outcomes.

Wisdom

To become wise is to harness appropriate resources and acquire the knowledge to contribute to what matters most. At our core, we believe that innovation management is a disciplined and strategic approach essential for driving business success and creating a global impact.

Patience

Patience empowers long-term vision. Embracing the time it takes to build impactful outcomes allows for reflection, iteration, and growth — foundations of sustainable success and meaningful innovation.

Luminarian (noun)
/ˈluː.mɪˌnɛəri.ən/

A forward-thinking leader who embodies the principles of ingenuity, intelligence, wisdom and patience.

Luminarians are distinguished executives recognized for their unique combination of talent, insight, and charisma. They drive transformation and inspire others by bringing innovative ideas to life and creating a profound impact on industries and global communities.
